BranchCounterPair getBranchCounterPair (const Stmt * S, Counter ParentCnt, optional SkipCntForOld)

Retrieve or assign the pair of Counter(s).

This returns BranchCounterPair {Executed, Skipped}.

Executed is the Counter associated with S assigned by an earlier

CounterMapping pass.

Skipped may be an expression (Executed - ParentCnt) or newly

assigned Counter in EnableSingleByteCoverage, as subtract

expressions are not available in this mode.

Parameters

S Key to the CounterMap
ParentCnt The Counter representing how many times S is evaluated.
SkipCntForOld (To be removed later) Optional fake Counter to override Skipped for adjustment of expressions in the old behavior of EnableSingleByteCoverage that is unaware of Branch coverage.

class NamedMDNode

===----------------------------------------------------------------------===//

A tuple of MDNodes.

Despite its name, a NamedMDNode isn't itself an MDNode.

NamedMDNodes are named module-level entities that contain lists of MDNodes.

It is illegal for a NamedMDNode to appear as an operand of an MDNode.

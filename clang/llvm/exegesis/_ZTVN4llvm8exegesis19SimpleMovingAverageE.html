Defined at line 26 of file llvm/tools/llvm-exegesis/lib/ProgressMeter.h

Represents `

{i=1..accumulated}{step_i} / accumulated`,

where `step_i` is the value passed to the `i`-th call to `step()`,

and `accumulated` is the total number of calls to `step()`.

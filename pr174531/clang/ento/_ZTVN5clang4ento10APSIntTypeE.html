void apply (llvm::APSInt & Value)

Convert a given APSInt, in place, to match this type.

This behaves like a C cast: converting 255u8 (0xFF) to s16 gives

255 (0x00FF), and converting -1s8 (0xFF) to u16 gives 65535 (0xFFFF).
